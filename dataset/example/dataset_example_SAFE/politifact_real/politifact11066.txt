Hillary Clinton Transcript: Building the ‘Growth and Fairness Economy’
Democratic presidential hopeful Hillary Clinton, laying out her economic vision, said Monday that government must do more to rein in excessive risk taking on Wall Street, push financial firms to focus on long-term growth over short-term gains and assure stock markets “work for everyday investors, not just high-frequency traders.” She also touched on the so-called sharing economy and its promise and pitfalls.
